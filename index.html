Hello, Welcome to Stereobyte!

I hope you enjoy your time here. :)

This is a personal blog of mine where I talk about my interests in music and counterculture. I created this website with help from W3 Schools and a few YouTube videos. I feel like making this website was a bit of a challenge, but it was a very fun process and I'd definitely do it again.

This website is a mix of a few of my favorite things. Purple, which is my absolute favorite color, I adore anything purple. Music, everything in my life is based in music. It makes me who I am and it means so much to me. And finally, nostalgia. This website is supposed to mimic the websites I used to use as a kid. It's my favorite website aesthetic and I think we should cherish old web design again, even if it's considered "ugly" or "outdated".
